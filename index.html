Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Welcome to BillBud. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience and to facilitate bill splitting and group management, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up, we collect your phone number, Google profile information (name, email, and profile picture), and UID to create and authenticate your account.
  • Contacts List: With your explicit local permission, we access your device's contacts list. This is exclusively used to help you find friends, verify registered users (viral loop checking), and add members to your split groups. We do not store your entire contact list on our servers.
  • Financial & Expense Data: We store the structured details of the expenses you create, including amounts, group members involved, categories, and settlement statuses.
  • Device & Usage Information: We may collect standard diagnostic data such as device type, OS version, and crash reports to improve app stability.
